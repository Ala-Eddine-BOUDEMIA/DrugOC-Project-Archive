## The Problem: The Burden of Adverse Drug Events

Adverse Drug Events (ADEs) are a major public health issue. However, reporting these events is often a slow and complex process for healthcare professionals. The goal of DrugOC was to create a modern, digital tool to streamline this process, improving the quality and speed of pharmacovigilance.

---

## The Core Challenge: Automating the French Imputability Method

The heart of the project was to digitally implement the official French method for causality assessment. The system I architected needed to calculate three distinct scores:

1.  **Informative Score (SI):** A preliminary score (NI0-NI2) to assess the quality of the available data for each case.
2.  **Intrinsic Imputability (I-Score):** The core clinical score based on chronological and semiological criteria.
3.  **Extrinsic Imputability (B-Score):** A bibliographic score (B1-B4) that quantifies whether the adverse event is already documented in scientific literature.
